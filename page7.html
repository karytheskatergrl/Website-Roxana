Hello!

My name is Roxana Botu and I am 24 years old. I am a freelancer designer from 2020 and I live in Barcelona. I work in a skate shop here as a graphic designer. In my spare time, I work as a freelancer. Over the years, I tried different types of programs and techniques and I can say that I can learn easily new things. So far, I worked in: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Blender, Sketch.

Timeline

24 September 2012

Liceul de Arte Plastice Nicolae Tonitza București

I started studying Interior Design in 2012 for four years at Liceul de Arte Plastice Nicolae Tonitza from Bucharest. 
I attended the high school of fine arts in Bucharest for 4 years.  Throughout these years, I learned different useful things that helped me to get my foundation in art. In the beginning, it seemed like a complicated thing to find my inspiration, but over the years it became a very easy thing. In those 4 years, I had the opportunity to learn from some of the most talented teachers in the country and to participate in some of the most important and internationally recognized art competitions. Also during high school, I decided that I needed to make a change and that it had to be in the direction of art. So, a year later, I was going to move to Cluj-Napoca and study at the best university in the country, specializing in interior design.

12 June 2019

Universitatea de Arte și Design Cluj-Napoca

In autumn 2016 I started my studies in Cluj. I chose my major graphic design which I studied for 3 years at the University of Art and Design. During my studies I managed to strengthen the information I learned in high school and started making my own works. That's why, during the university I had to realize different projects, using different techniques. Thus, I learned to work in programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Blender, Rhinoceros 3D, 3D Max. In addition, I started to present my projects at various well-known exhibitions in the city, including: Open Days at the Art Museum. After my three years of studies, I graduated from university in 2019. For my degree project, I made the game Geosphere. Future plans I didn't have yet, but I knew it was harder to make a name for yourself in art, so I waited.

1 January 2020

Freelancer designer 

At the end of 2019, I decided to move out of the country, so I moved to Barcelona. Leaving behind all my friends, family and acquaintances, I knew it was going to be a huge challenge for me, but something told me this was what I had to do. So, in the fall of 2019, I packed my bags and bought a one-way plane ticket to Barcelona. Arriving there, not knowing the language and only coping with Duolingo, I started to think that maybe it was a mistake. However, with hard work, I managed to find various remote jobs in the arts. The bad part was that only sometimes I would get projects, and the rest of the time I would stay and look for a more suitable place. I finally found, after several failed attempts, a skateshop looking for a graphic designer. I have been working in this skateshop for 2 years and in my spare time, I do different art projects.
